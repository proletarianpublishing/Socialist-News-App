# Socialist-News-App
A news app for android that exclusively carries socialist and anti-imperialist publications

Lightweight Android app that's designed to provide a fast and simple reading experience without accounts, subscriptions, tracking, or unnecessary bloat.

---

## Features

- Separate Canada, UK, US, and World news tabs
- News feed aggregation
- Lightweight and fast interface
- Dark mode support
- Minimal permissions needed
- No account required
- No analytics or tracking

4. Complete installation

## Requirements

- Android 6.0 (API 24) or newer

## Privacy

This application:

- Does not collect personal data
- Does not use analytics
- Does not use advertising SDKs
- Does not require accounts or login
- Does not track user behaviour
